Trip to Lucerne, Switzerland

This photo was taken during my first trip to Switzerland. Lucerne is a very beautiful city in central Switzerland, in the German-speaking portion of the country. With a population of about 80,000 people, Lucerne is the most populous town in Central Switzerland, and a nexus of economics, transportation, culture, and media of this region.

Trip to Schangnau, Switzerland

We visited a small village in Schangnau. The mountains around Schangnau provided timber, meadows for the villagers' cattle. The cows and sheep were friendly and seemed very curious about us when we walked by.
