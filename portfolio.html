Trip to Lucerne, Switzerland

This was my first trip to Switzerland. Lucerne is a very beautiful city in central Switzerland, in the German-speaking portion of the country. With a population of about 80,000 people, Lucerne is the most populous town in Central Switzerland, and a nexus of economics, transportation, culture, and media of this region.
